Derive key with subkey identified by a byte array presented as a hash, and a chain code.

At present, your only valid type paramater choices might be sha3::Shake128/126, which we explain further in lib.rs by the extern crate sha3; line. There remain sitautions where passing the hash will prove more convenient than managing strings however.
